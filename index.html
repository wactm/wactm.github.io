West African Center for Tropical Mycology

The West African Center for tropical Mycology (WACTM) is a planed research center for mycological research being developed at University of Parakou, Parakou, Benin. The center development is a collaboration between researchers at the University of Parkou and the Uppsala University (Sweden). The mission is to further the mycological research in Western Africa ito promote science and society.
